About Stratum

An operational technology partner for growing BC businesses.

Stratum is a structured, security-aware managed technology partner serving the Lower Mainland. We are built for organizations that need reliable systems, clear accountability, and long-term stewardship — without the overhead of an enterprise IT department.

Talk With Stratum →
Our story

Stratum exists because the middle was missing.

Growing organizations in BC kept ending up in the same place — too complex for break-fix IT, too small to justify deep internal IT teams, and underserved by generic MSP offers. We built Stratum to be the structured operational partner that actually fits this gap. Not a vendor selling tools. Not a help desk on standby. A partner responsible for the environment over time.

Values

What we operate by

Structure, Security, Stability, Simplicity, Stewardship.

The 5S lens is the filter for our service model, our proposals, our internal decisions, and how we show up for clients every day.

Plain language

We translate technical issues into business decisions — without dumbing them down.

Direct communication

Status updates, expectations, and tradeoffs are stated clearly, not hidden in tickets.

Long-term thinking

We act as if we will be supporting your environment three and five years from now — because we plan to.

Calm under pressure

Outages, incidents, and emergencies are handled with structure — not panic.
