# 1B. Product Maturity View

This table shows where each service area sits today and what needs to happen next.

| Service Area | Status | Today | Future Direction | Main Gap |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Managed IT Support** | Current | Active support service through tickets, remote support, onsite work, dispatch, and escalation. | Structured managed IT plans. | Packaging, pricing, and clear plan inclusions. |
| **Server, Network, and Infrastructure** | Current | Active support for servers, networks, firewalls, Wi-Fi, patching, and documentation. | Lifecycle-managed infrastructure. | Roadmaps, replacement planning, and reporting. |
| **Backup and Recovery** | Current / Emerging | Backup checks, test restores, recovery work, and backup troubleshooting. | Backup assurance and recovery planning. | Testing rhythm, documentation, reporting, and client-facing recovery expectations. |
| **Security and Cybersecurity** | Current / Emerging | Incident response, MFA, access control, endpoint protection, email security, patching, monitoring, and alert response. | Managed security packages and cyber insurance readiness. | Ownership, stack, standards, reporting, and pricing. |
| **Hardware, Purchasing, and Setup** | Current | Hardware purchasing, device setup, configuration, and sales. | Standardized device lifecycle and shop structure. | Approved catalog, process, lifecycle planning, and procurement standards. |
| **Project and Migration Work** | Current / Emerging | Server migrations, cloud migrations, infrastructure upgrades, and planned technical changes. | Formal project delivery model. | Intake, scope, schedule, communication, closeout, and handoff. |
| **Consulting and Strategy** | Emerging | Technology reviews, roadmap conversations, advisory sessions, and recommendations. | Technology roadmap and QBR product. | Format, cadence, ownership, and account management rhythm. |
| **Software, Automation, and Custom Development** | Emerging / Future | Software engineering, automation, integrations, internal tools, and client-specific support. | Business systems implementation services. | Methodology, delivery limits, ownership, and support model. |
| **Account Management and Client Success** | Emerging / Future | Relationship management is happening, but mostly through service and leadership. | Formal client success program. | Role ownership, monthly reports, QBRs, lifecycle planning, and customer rhythm. |
