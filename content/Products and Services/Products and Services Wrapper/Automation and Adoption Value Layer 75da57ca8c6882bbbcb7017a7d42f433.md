# Automation and Adoption Value Layer

## Purpose

This page captures the idea that automation and adoption must become part of Stratum’s value story, not just support tickets.

## Why it matters

Clients do not only need problems fixed.

They also need:

- better use of the systems they already pay for
- more consistent workflows
- less dependence on manual workarounds
- better adoption of Microsoft 365, CRM, ERP, AI, and automation tools

## What this could include

- Microsoft 365 workflow improvement
- Power Automate opportunities
- ERP / CRM implementation support
- AI and automation use-case guidance
- adoption support and training
- process simplification work tied to real operations

## How it fits the package line

| Package | Automation / Adoption role |
| --- | --- |
| Essentials | Limited; mostly tool setup and support |
| Business | Workflow improvement opportunities begin here |
| Secure | Adoption plus stronger governance and risk awareness |
| Complete | Ongoing modernization, automation, and advisory-led improvement |

## Strategic note

This layer helps Stratum move from only support and maintenance into higher-value operational improvement work.
