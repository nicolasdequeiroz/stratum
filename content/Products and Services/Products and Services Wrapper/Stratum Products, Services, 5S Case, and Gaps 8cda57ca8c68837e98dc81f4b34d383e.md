# Stratum Products, Services, 5S Case, and Gaps

**Internal Working Draft**

This document is the first pass at organizing Stratum’s current services, future services, and gaps through the 5S brand lens.

The goal is not to create a finished product catalogue yet. The goal is to get clear on what we provide today, what we want to build toward, and what needs to be tightened before this becomes website copy, pricing, packages, or sales material.

## Executive Summary

Stratum already provides many of the services required to become a more structured managed technology partner.

The issue is not that we need to invent everything from scratch. The issue is that we need to organize what we already do, package it properly, and build the missing layers around account management, security and cybersecurity, backup assurance, project delivery, business systems, and client strategy.

## Working Frame

We need to get clear on what Stratum provides today, what we want to build toward, and what the gaps are.

The point is not to make a huge product list for the sake of it. The point is to create structure around the work we already do, then decide what needs to become a proper service, package, process, or future revenue line.

Our core brand lens is:

| Lens | Meaning for Stratum |
| --- | --- |
| **Structure** | We organize technology, process, support, and accountability so clients are not operating in chaos. |
| **Security** | We protect clients from cyber risk, operational disruption, data loss, and avoidable exposure. |
| **Stability** | We build and maintain dependable systems that reduce downtime, surprises, and reactive firefighting. |
| **Simplicity** | We make technology easier to understand, easier to use, and easier to make decisions around. |
| **Stewardship** | We act as a long-term technology partner responsible for protecting the client’s best interests. |

Every product and service should be able to connect back to one or more of those words. If it does not, we either need to clarify it, improve it, or stop making it a focus.
