# 4. Stratum Complete

### Positioning

**Full technology partnership for clients that want Stratum involved in planning, reporting, security, lifecycle, roadmap, budgeting, and long-term technology accountability.**

### Target market

| Best Fit | Not Best Fit |
| --- | --- |
| Mid-sized and larger businesses | Small clients only needing simple support |
| Leadership wants reporting and accountability | Clients unwilling to engage in planning |
| Client has multiple systems, locations, servers, or departments | Clients only buying lowest-price IT |
| Client needs technology roadmap and budgeting | Clients not ready for recurring reviews |
| Client wants Stratum as long-term technology partner | Clients that treat IT as occasional repair work |
