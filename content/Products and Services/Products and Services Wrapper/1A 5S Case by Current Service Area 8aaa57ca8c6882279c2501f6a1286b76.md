# 1A. 5S Case by Current Service Area

The 5S Case is how we explain why each service matters.

Each service should be able to answer:

- **Structure:** How does this organize the customer’s technology, process, support, or accountability?
- **Security:** How does this reduce cyber risk, operational disruption, data loss, or exposure?
- **Stability:** How does this reduce downtime, surprises, or reactive firefighting?
- **Simplicity:** How does this make technology easier to understand, use, or make decisions around?
- **Stewardship:** How does this protect the customer’s best interests today and into the future?

---

## 5S Case: Managed IT Support

| 5S Lens | Case |
| --- | --- |
| **Structure** | Managed IT Support gives the customer a clear support system: tickets, priorities, dispatch, escalation, ownership, and follow-up. |
| **Security** | Support issues are handled through a controlled process instead of random workarounds, unmanaged access, or undocumented changes. |
| **Stability** | Problems are resolved faster, repeated issues are identified, and the customer has fewer interruptions to their workday. |
| **Simplicity** | The customer knows where to go, who to contact, and how support will be handled. |
| **Stewardship** | We are not just fixing today’s ticket. We are learning the customer’s environment and protecting their long-term technology interests. |

| --- | --- |
